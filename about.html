Who We Are

QaShift is an independent QA learning platform focused on sharing practical knowledge about software testing, test automation, and related tools. The content is written in plain language with step-by-step guides that help learners grow from beginner to advanced levels.

Our Mission

The mission of QaShift is to make quality assurance concepts easy to understand, well-structured, and accessible to everyone. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world testing practices.

Topics We Cover

The website offers learning resources on:

  • Manual Testing
  • Selenium WebDriver (Java-based)
  • Playwright for Automation
  • Agile and Scrum Basics
  • JIRA for Test Management
  • Test Case Design
  • TestNG, Page Object Model, and more

About the Creator

QaShift is created and maintained by Vinay Shenoy, a Quality Assurance enthusiast with practical experience in manual and automation testing. This website is a personal initiative to share learning with others in a clear and structured format.

Content Policy

All content published on QaShift is original and manually written. No copied or AI-generated material is used. Every guide is based on hands-on practice to ensure it is useful and reliable for learners and professionals alike.
